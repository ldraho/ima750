Street Trees of New York City

A collection of street tree beds across New York City.

Street tree beds need to be kept clean of litter so that trees can thrive. The City does not clean trash from street tree beds, but they should. Help raise awareness for better street tree care by taking photos of street trees in your area and upload the photos to Instgram and tag @nyctreelove.

Help take care of the trees in your area by visiting the resources page to find about tree care events and workshops.
